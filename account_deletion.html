Account Deletion for PrepQuest

To delete your account in PrepQuest, please follow these steps:

  • Open the PrepQuest app on your device.
  • Tap the "Settings" button in the bottom right corner of the screen.
  • Scroll down and tap the "Delete Account" button.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the deletion of your account.

Please note that once you delete your account, all of your data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
