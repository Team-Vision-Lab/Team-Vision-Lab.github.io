Privacy Policy for PrepQuest

This Privacy Policy outlines how PrepQuest collects, uses, and shares personal information from its users. By using PrepQuest, the user consents to the practices described in this policy.

Information Collected

PrepQuest collects various types of personal information, which may include:

  • Account Information: When a user creates an account on PrepQuest, PrepQuest collects the user's name and email address. However, creating an account is not mandatory to use PrepQuest.
  • Advertisements: PrepQuest uses AdMob to serve advertisements to users. AdMob is an advertising service provided by Google LLC. AdMob may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about user preferences and interests to serve targeted ads. This information may include device information, location data, and user interactions with PrepQuest.
  • Speech-to-Text Capability: PrepQuest allows users to record audio to be transcribed into text. This audio is temporarily stored on the device and is not shared with third parties.

Use of Information

PrepQuest uses the user's personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve services to the user.
  • To personalize the user's experience with PrepQuest.
  • To communicate with the user about PrepQuest and related services.
  • To send promotional materials and advertisements to the user.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Sharing of Information

PrepQuest does not sell, rent, or lease the user's personal information to third parties. PrepQuest may share the user's information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers: PrepQuest may share the user's information with third-party service providers who assist with business operations.
  • For legal reasons: PrepQuest may share the user's information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or subpoena.
  • With user consent: PrepQuest may share the user's information with their consent.

Data Retention

PrepQuest retains the user's personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the services requested or for other essential purposes such as complying with legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing policies.

Security

PrepQuest takes reasonable measures to protect the user's personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, and misuse. However, no security system is completely secure, and PrepQuest cannot guarantee the security of the user's information.
